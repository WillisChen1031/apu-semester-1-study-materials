## Week 1: Digital Generation / 数字世代

### 1. Digital Generation / 数字世代

- English: Digital Generation mainly refers to Generation Z, people growing up constantly connected through smartphones, tablets, and computers.
- 中文：数字世代主要指 Z 世代，他们从小通过智能手机、平板电脑和电脑与世界保持持续连接。
- English: Because of hyperconnectivity, this generation learns, thinks, acts, and responds differently from previous generations.
- 中文：因为高度互联，他们的学习方式、思考方式、行为方式和回应方式都不同于上一代。

### 2. Millennials vs Gen Z / 千禧一代与 Z 世代

- English: Both Millennials and Gen Z are shaped by technology and mostly live in a mobile-first world.
- 中文：千禧一代和 Z 世代都受到科技塑造，并且多数生活在移动优先的环境中。
- English: They differ in how they shop, interact with brands, and view money.
- 中文：他们在购物方式、与品牌互动的方式、对金钱的看法上明显不同。
- English: Gen Z is described as a throwback generation that wants to work, save money, and avoid being trapped by credit.
- 中文：Z 世代被描述为一种“回归型世代”，他们希望工作、存钱，并避免被信用债务困住。

### 3. Characteristics of Gen Z / Z 世代特征

- Insightful and Savvy / 有洞察力且精明：Gen Z has access to vast information and world knowledge, can identify insincere marketing, and expects transparency and social responsibility.
- 中文：Z 世代能接触大量信息和世界知识，能看穿不真诚的营销，并期待透明度和社会责任。
- Responsible Self-Starters / 负责任且主动：They are driven, hardworking, responsible, and see technology as a catalyst for achieving goals.
- 中文：他们有动力、努力、负责任，并把科技看成实现目标的催化剂。
- Risk-Averse and Adaptable / 风险规避且适应力强：They understand change can happen quickly, weigh purchases practically, and often have a savings-conscious mindset.
- 中文：他们知道变化可能很快发生，会实际评估消费价值，并通常有储蓄意识。
- Tech-Enabled / 科技赋能：They access computers and smartphones early, are digital natives, learn quickly, and often solve problems independently.
- 中文：他们很早接触电脑和智能手机，是数字原住民，学习速度快，并常能自主解决问题。

### 4. Stereotypes and Reinterpretation / 刻板印象与重新理解

- English: Short attention span can also mean concise communication.
- 中文：注意力时间短也可以被理解为沟通简洁高效。
- English: Multitasking may increase stress but can also help in chaotic environments.
- 中文：多任务处理可能增加压力，但也可能帮助他们适应混乱环境。
- English: Technology dependence does not mean they cannot mentor others; they may become technology mentors in workplaces.
- 中文：依赖科技不代表他们无法帮助别人；他们也可能在职场成为科技导师。
- English: Job-hopping and high expectations mean employers need strong benefits, company culture, and career growth opportunities.
- 中文：跳槽倾向和高期待意味着雇主需要提供更好的福利、企业文化和职业成长机会。

## Week 4: Systems Thinking and Organizational Innovation / 系统思维与组织创新

### 1. Systems Thinking / 系统思维

- English: Systems Thinking is an approach to integration based on the belief that parts of a system act differently when isolated from the system environment or other parts.
- 中文：系统思维是一种整合方法，它认为系统组件一旦脱离系统环境或其他部分，其行为会不同。
- English: It studies linkages and interactions between elements that form the whole system.
- 中文：它关注构成整体系统的各元素之间的联系和互动。

### 2. Systems Thinking vs Analysis / 系统思维与分析思维

- English: Analysis breaks things into smaller parts; systems thinking examines wholes and relationships.
- 中文：分析思维把事物拆成小部分；系统思维关注整体和关系。
- English: Pure analysis gives a limited understanding if relationships between parts are ignored.
- 中文：如果忽略各部分之间的关系，单纯分析会导致对现实的理解有限。
- English: Systems thinking sees events as dynamic, connected, and influenced by location within the system.
- 中文：系统思维把事件看作动态、相互连接，并受系统中所处位置影响。

### 3. Why Systems Thinking Matters / 为什么系统思维重要

- English: People often make incomplete assumptions about cause and outcome, then repeat the same problem-solving treadmill.
- 中文：人们常对因果关系作出不完整假设，因此反复陷入同样的问题循环。
- English: Systems thinking helps challenge mental models, consider long-term and short-term impacts, and ask probing questions.
- 中文：系统思维帮助人们挑战心智模型，同时考虑长期与短期影响，并提出深入问题。

### 4. Basic Characteristics of a System / 系统基本特征

- Heap or System / 堆还是系统：A heap does not fundamentally change when parts are added or removed; a system changes when parts are added or removed.
- 中文：堆增减部分后本质不变；系统增减部分后整体功能会改变。
- Whole greater than sum / 整体大于部分之和：Interactions among parts create emergent properties.
- 中文：部分之间的互动会产生单个部分相加无法得到的涌现属性。
- Purpose / 目的：Systems usually have a purpose, and subsystems may have conflicting purposes.
- 中文：系统通常有目的，子系统之间的目的可能冲突。
- Circular causality / 循环因果：Feedback loops mean A affects B, B affects C, and C may return to affect A.
- 中文：反馈回路意味着事件之间可能循环影响。
- Systems archetypes / 系统原型：Different situations may show similar patterns, such as escalation.
- 中文：不同情境可能呈现相似的系统模式，例如竞争升级。

### 5. Systems Thinking for Organizations / 组织中的系统思维

- English: It helps organizations understand changing conditions, involve stakeholders, identify problems, and support transformational change.
- 中文：它帮助组织理解变化环境、整合利益相关者、识别问题并支持转型变革。
- Benefits / 好处：
  - explore new business opportunities / 探索新商业机会。
  - create compelling vision of the future / 建立有吸引力的未来愿景。
  - understand complex human factors / 理解复杂的人为因素。
  - redesign broken systems / 重新设计失效系统。

### 6. Sustainable Development Goals / 可持续发展目标

- Level 1 / 第一层：Joined-up efforts on individual goals.
- 中文：对单个目标进行协同努力。
- Level 2 / 第二层：A network set of goals, seeing inter-relationships between goals.
- 中文：把目标看成网络，理解目标之间的相互关系。
- Level 3 / 第三层：The how of sustainable development, delivering goals in a way that models sustainable society.
- 中文：关注实现方式，以符合可持续社会特征的方式达成目标。

  - higher record-keeping and transparency / 更高水平的记录保存和透明度。

- 中文：加密货币解决了双重支付问题，其开端是比特币。

## Topic 11: Digital Security / 数字安全

### 1. Digital Security Risk / 数字安全风险

- English: Digital Security Risk is an event that could cause loss of or damage to hardware, software, data, information, or processing capability.
- 中文：数字安全风险是可能导致硬件、软件、数据、信息或处理能力损失或损坏的事件。
- Computer Crime / 计算机犯罪：illegal act involving a computer or related devices.
- 中文：涉及计算机或相关设备的非法行为。
- Cybercrime / 网络犯罪：online or Internet-based illegal act.
- 中文：在线或基于互联网的非法行为。

### 2. Malware Types / 恶意软件类型

- Adware / 广告软件：displays online advertisements in banners, pop-ups, or other internet services.
- 中文：通过横幅、弹窗或其他互联网服务展示在线广告。
- Ransomware / 勒索软件：blocks or limits access until the user pays money.

- Rootkit / Rootkit：hides in a device and allows remote full control.

- Spyware / 间谍软件：secretly collects user information and sends it outside.
- 中文：秘密收集用户信息并传送给外部来源。
- Trojan Horse / 特洛伊木马：hides within or looks like a legitimate program; does not replicate itself.
- 中文：隐藏在合法程序中或伪装成合法程序；不会自我复制。
- Virus / 病毒：damaging program that infects a device and alters how it works.
- 中文：感染设备并改变其工作方式的破坏性程序。
- Worm / 蠕虫：copies itself repeatedly, consuming resources and possibly shutting down devices or networks.
- 中文：反复自我复制、消耗资源，可能导致设备或网络关闭。
- Payload / 有害载荷：destructive event delivered when opening infected files/programs or booting with infected media.
- 中文：打开感染文件/程序或用感染介质启动时触发的破坏性事件。
- Macro Virus / 宏病毒：malicious code written in word-processing or spreadsheet macros.
- 中文：写在文字处理或电子表格宏中的恶意代码。

### 3. Internet and Network Attacks / 互联网与网络攻击

- Botnet / 僵尸网络：group of compromised computers or mobile devices connected to a network.
- 中文：由被攻陷的电脑或移动设备组成的网络。
- Zombie PC / 僵尸电脑：a compromised computer or device.
- 中文：被攻陷的电脑或设备。
- Denial of Service (DoS) / 拒绝服务攻击：disrupts access to an Internet service.
- 中文：破坏用户对互联网服务的访问。
- Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) / 分布式拒绝服务攻击：larger-scale DoS attack using multiple computers/networks.
- 中文：利用多台电脑/多个网络发动的大规模拒绝服务攻击。
- Backdoor / 后门：program or instructions that allow users to bypass security controls.
- 中文：让用户绕过安全控制的程序或指令。

### 4. Preventive Measures / 防护措施

- Install antivirus software / 安装杀毒软件。
- Install a personal firewall / 安装个人防火墙。
- Scan removable media / 扫描可移动介质。
- Never open attachments from unknown or untrusted senders / 不打开未知或不可信发件人的附件。
- Delete infected attachments immediately / 立即删除被标记感染的附件。
- Never start a computer with removable media / 不使用可移动介质启动电脑。
- Set macro security to enable or disable macros / 设置宏安全等级以控制宏启用。
- Check downloaded programs for viruses, worms, or Trojan horses / 检查下载程序是否含病毒、蠕虫或木马。

### 5. Antivirus and Virus Signature / 杀毒软件与病毒特征码

- Antivirus / 杀毒软件：originally developed to detect and remove viruses; also protects against worms and Trojan horses.
- 中文：最初用于检测和移除病毒，也能防护蠕虫和木马。
- Virus Signature / 病毒特征码：specific binary pattern of virus code used by antivirus programs to detect viruses.
- 中文：病毒代码的特定二进制模式，杀毒软件用它识别病毒。

### 6. System Failure and Power Protection / 系统故障与电力保护

- Undervoltage / 欠压：drop in electrical supply.
- 中文：电力供应下降。
- Overvoltage or Power Surge / 过压或电涌：significant increase in electrical power.
- 中文：电力大幅增加。
- Noise / 电噪声：unwanted electrical signal.
- 中文：不需要的电信号干扰。
- UPS / 不间断电源：surge protector that provides power during power loss.
- 中文：在断电时提供电力的电涌保护设备。

### 7. Digital Signature, Digital Certificate, CA, SSL / 数字签名、数字证书、证书机构、SSL

- Digital Signature / 数字签名：encrypted code attached to an electronic message to verify sender identity.
- 中文：附加在电子消息上的加密代码，用于验证发送者身份。
- Digital Certificate / 数字证书：notice that guarantees a user or website is legitimate.
- 中文：用于保证用户或网站合法性的证明。
- Certificate Authority (CA) / 证书颁发机构：authorized company that issues and verifies digital certificates.
- 中文：负责颁发和验证数字证书的授权公司。
- Secure Socket Layer (SSL) / 安全套接层：encrypts data between client and internet server; `https` indicates secure connection.
- 中文：加密客户端与互联网服务器之间的数据；`https` 表示安全连接。

### 8. Backup and Disaster Recovery / 备份与灾难恢复

- Backup / 备份：duplicate file, program, or disk.
- 中文：复制文件、程序或磁盘。
- Full Backup / 完整备份：back up all files.
- 中文：备份所有文件。
- Selective Backup / 选择性备份：select files to back up.
- 中文：选择部分文件备份。
- Disaster Recovery Plan / 灾难恢复计划：written plan for restoring computer operations after disaster.
- 中文：灾难后恢复计算机运行的书面计划。
- Emergency Plan / 应急计划：steps immediately after disaster.
- 中文：灾难发生后立即采取的步骤。
- Backup Plan / 备份计划：how backup files/equipment resume processing.
- 中文：如何使用备份文件和设备恢复信息处理。
- Recovery Plan / 恢复计划：actions to restore full information processing.
- 中文：恢复完整信息处理操作的行动。
- Test Plan / 测试计划：simulate disaster levels and record recovery ability.
- 中文：模拟不同灾难级别并记录恢复能力。

## Topic 12: Digital Ethics / 数字伦理

### 1. Computer Ethics / 计算机伦理

- English: Computer Ethics are moral guidelines that govern the use of computers and information systems.
- 中文：计算机伦理是规范计算机和信息系统使用的道德准则。
- Ethical IT concerns / IT 伦理问题：
  - workplace email and internet monitoring / 工作场所电子邮件与互联网访问监控。
  - software piracy and copyright violation / 软件盗版和违反版权下载。
  - unsolicited email, spoofing, spamming, phishing / 垃圾邮件、欺骗、群发垃圾邮件、网络钓鱼。
  - hacker and cracker theft / 黑客和破解者盗窃。
  - student plagiarism / 学生抄袭。
  - malware, cookies, spyware tracking / 恶意软件、cookie、间谍软件非法追踪。

### 2. Morality, Ethics, Law / 道德、伦理与法律

- Morality / 道德：shared social conventions and beliefs about right and wrong behavior.
- 中文：社会共同认可的关于正确与错误行为的约定和信念。
- Ethics / 伦理：principles guiding right behavior, especially in professional or organizational contexts.
- 中文：指导正确行为的原则，尤其用于专业或组织情境。
- Law / 法律：formal rules enforced by society/government.
- 中文：由社会或政府执行的正式规则。
- Virtue / 美德：habit that inclines people to do what is acceptable.
- 中文：使人倾向于做可接受行为的习惯。
- Vice / 恶习：habit of unacceptable behavior.
- 中文：不可接受行为的习惯。

### 3. Code of Ethics / 道德准则

- English: A Code of Ethics documents an organization's mission, values, professional problem-solving principles, and expected behavior.
- 中文：道德准则记录组织使命、价值观、专业人员处理问题的原则和预期行为。
- English: Breaking the code may result in termination.
- 中文：违反道德准则可能导致被解雇。
- Why important / 重要性：It lays rules for behavior and provides preemptive warning.
- 中文：它明确行为规则，并提前警示员工。

### 4. ACM Code Examples / ACM 道德准则例子

- Avoid harm to others / 避免伤害他人。
- Be fair and do not discriminate / 公平并避免歧视。
- Honor property rights / 尊重财产权。
- Give proper credit for intellectual property / 正确认可知识产权。
- Respect privacy / 尊重隐私。
- Honor confidentiality / 尊重保密性。

### 5. Professionalism and Integrity / 专业精神与诚信

- Profession / 专业：requires specialized knowledge and intensive academic preparation.
- 中文：需要专门知识和深入学术准备。
- Professionals / 专业人士：have advanced training and experience, exercise judgment, and keep up with developments.
- 中文：具有高级培训和经验，能运用判断力，并持续跟进领域发展。
- Integrity / 诚信：acting according to personal principles while respecting others and moral values.
- 中文：按照个人原则行动，同时尊重他人和道德价值。

### 6. Business Ethics and CSR / 商业伦理与企业社会责任

- English: Complex workplaces, pressure for revenue/profit, financial loss, and lawsuits increase unethical behavior risk.
- 中文：复杂工作环境、收入/利润压力、财务损失和诉讼风险会增加不道德行为的可能。
- Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) / 企业社会责任：organization takes responsibility for impacts on environment, community, and employee welfare.
- 中文：组织对其行为对环境、社区和员工福利造成的影响承担责任。

### 7. Ethics Training / 伦理培训

- Goal / 目标：
  - Teach employees how to apply code of ethics in real life / 教员工如何在现实中应用道德准则。
  - Encourage reporting misconduct / 鼓励举报不当行为。
  - Show effective reporting methods / 展示有效举报方式。
  - Reassure no retaliation / 保证举报者不会遭到报复。

### 8. Ethical Decisions / 伦理决策

- English: When dealing with computer crime, a firm may need to decide whether to prosecute, maintain low profile, or inform affected customers.
- 中文：面对计算机犯罪时，公司可能需要决定是否起诉、是否保持低调、是否通知受影响客户。
- English: Firms must decide resources for safeguards and actions when vulnerable software is found.
- 中文：公司必须决定投入多少资源进行安全防护，以及发现软件易受攻击时应采取什么行动。

### 9. Ethical Issues / 伦理问题

- Software Piracy / 软件盗版：unauthorized copying of software; BSA aims to stop it.
- 中文：未经授权复制软件；BSA 致力于阻止软件盗版。
- Trade Secret / 商业秘密：information with economic value, development cost/effort, and uniqueness or novelty.
- 中文：具有经济价值、需要开发成本或努力，并具有一定独特性或新颖性的信息。
- Whistle-blowing / 吹哨/举报：employee effort to expose negligent, illegal, unethical, or abusive company acts threatening public interest.
- 中文：员工揭露威胁公共利益的疏忽、违法、不道德或滥用行为。
- Conflict of Interest / 利益冲突：employee self-interest conflicts with client interest.
- 中文：员工个人利益与客户利益发生冲突。
- Fraud / 欺诈：obtaining goods, services, or property through deception.
- 中文：通过欺骗获取商品、服务或财产。
- Misrepresentation / 虚假陈述：misstatement or incomplete statement of material fact.
- 中文：对重要事实作出错误或不完整陈述。
- Breach of Contract / 违约：one party fails to meet contract terms.
- 中文：一方未履行合同条款。
- Bribes and Gifts / 贿赂与礼物：exam may ask how to differentiate them.
- 中文：考试可能要求区分贿赂和礼物。

| Digital Signature | 数字签名 | 用于验证电子消息发送者身份的加密代码。 |
| Digital Certificate | 数字证书 | 保证用户或网站合法性的证明。 |
| Certificate Authority (CA) | 证书颁发机构 | 颁发和验证数字证书的授权机构。 |
| Secure Socket Layer (SSL) | 安全套接层 | 加密客户端与服务器之间数据的技术。 |
| Backup | 备份 | 复制文件、程序或磁盘。 |
| Disaster Recovery Plan | 灾难恢复计划 | 灾难后恢复计算机运行的书面计划。 |
| Computer Ethics | 计算机伦理 | 规范计算机和信息系统使用的道德准则。 |
| Morality | 道德 | 社会关于正确与错误行为的共同信念。 |
| Ethics | 伦理 | 指导正确行为的原则。 |
| Law | 法律 | 由政府或社会执行的正式规则。 |
| Code of Ethics | 道德准则 | 组织对价值观和行为规则的正式说明。 |
| Professionalism | 专业精神 | 专业人员应具备的知识、判断和持续学习态度。 |
| Integrity | 诚信 | 按个人原则和道德价值行动。 |
| Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) | 企业社会责任 | 组织对环境、社区和员工福利影响负责。 |
| Software Piracy | 软件盗版 | 未经授权复制软件。 |
| Trade Secret | 商业秘密 | 具有经济价值和独特性的商业信息。 |
| Whistle-blowing | 吹哨/举报 | 员工揭露组织的不当、违法或不道德行为。 |
| Conflict of Interest | 利益冲突 | 个人利益与客户或组织利益冲突。 |
| Fraud | 欺诈 | 通过欺骗获取利益。 |
| Misrepresentation | 虚假陈述 | 对重要事实进行错误或不完整陈述。 |
| Breach of Contract | 违约 | 未履行合同条款。 |
| Bribe | 贿赂 | 为获得不正当利益而给予的利益。 |
| Gift | 礼物 | 正当社交或商业礼节中的赠与，考试可能要求与贿赂区分。 |
